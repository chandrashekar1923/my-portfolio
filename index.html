Recent Projects

Project 1

Food Rescue

This project is used to manage wastage foods in a useful way. Every day the people are wasting lots of foods. So we have to reduce that food wastage problem through online. If anyone have wastage foods they are entering their food quantity details and their address in that application and then the admin maintain the details of food donator. The donator can create the account and whenever they are having wastage food they can login and give request to the admin. And the admin also maintain the buyer(orphanage, poor people,..) details too. After the admin view the donator request and give the alert message like time to come and collect the food. And the admin collect foods from donator people. After receiving the food from the agent by admin and give alert message to that donator.This project is food redistribution is an enormously successful social innovation that tackles food waste and food poverty.

Project 2

Fake Image Detection

Images play a very important role in understanding the things. Back then, there were no proper technologies used for morphing of images but now due to the rapid development in the technology there have been much advancement in image modification tools. So, when an image is being uploaded, people started to edit these images and use for them for their own demands. Social media is the best platform for collecting the images that are to be morphed and for reuploading them. This not only causes emotional damage of the person but also creates a bad review in the society. So, the objective of our project is that it identifies whether the images of human face are real or fake. Initially the system is trained with the images of human face for further classification of real or fake. In this project we are using LBP algorithm to detect the human of image fake or real using CNN classification.
